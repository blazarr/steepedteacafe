About

From our family to yours

We are a family owned restaurant with a dream of being able to bring other families together over a heart warming cup of tea.

Each pastry, soup and sandwich is made in-house daily, and paired with an assortment of teas that will bring your palate to life. If you’re out and about, or just looking for a relaxing afternoon, treat yourself to a well-earned tasty treat from our family to yours.
